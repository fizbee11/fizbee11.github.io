# Real Estate Property Management in ERPNext
Implemented ERPNext for a real estate company to manage it's operations like renting, maintenance, leasing, contracting. 

Developed custom ERPNext App and customized Utility Billing modules for customized taxation as per tenant type, ownership type, automated VAT, Witholding Taxes as well as other regulatory compliance in Tanzania.

### Features
As per customer's requirements, the following features were developed: 
- **Taxation**: 
  - Automation for **VAT** % according to Tenant Type: Residential or Commercial
  - **Witholding Taxes**: Automation to ensure Witholding taxes applies only to applicable Invoices.
- **Rent 0r Service Charge**: Automation to charge Rent or Service charge as per tenant type.
- **Generate Future Invoices**: One click generation of Draft/submitted Sales invoices for upcoming months.
- **Forecast Cash Inflow Report**.
- **Customized print templates for Sales Invoice and Quotations.**
- **Custom billing cycle rented properties.**
